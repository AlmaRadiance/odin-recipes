Grilled Chicken Salad Recipe

Grilled Chicken Salad

Indulge in the delightful flavors of this grilled chicken salad featuring juicy tomatoes, crisp lettuce, and a medley of fresh vegetables. The grilled chicken adds a smoky char, while the tangy balsamic vinaigrette ties everything together for a balanced and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 6 cups mixed lettuce leaves (e.g., romaine, iceberg, red leaf)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts or almonds (optional)
  • /4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. In a bowl, mix olive oil, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and black pepper. Brush the chicken breasts with the oil and herb mixture. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes on each side, or until cooked through and no longer pink in the center. Remove from the grill and let the chicken rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.

Step 2

In a large salad bowl, toss the mixed lettuce leaves, cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, red onion slices, and bell pepper slices. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese and chopped nuts (if using) over the salad ingredients.

Step 3

In a small bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar, extra-virgin olive oil, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, honey (if using), salt, and black pepper until well combined. Arrange the sliced grilled chicken on top of the salad. Drizzle the balsamic vinaigrette over the salad. Gently toss the salad to evenly distribute the ingredients and dressing. Divide the grilled chicken salad among plates or bowls. Serve immediately, optionally garnishing with extra feta cheese and nuts if desired.
