Cobb Salad Recipe

Cobb Salad

The Cobb salad is a hearty and flavorful dish that brings together a medley of ingredients, offering a balance of textures and tastes. With a variety of toppings, this salad is a meal in itself, perfect for a satisfying lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed salad greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula, etc.)
  • 2 cooked chicken breasts, diced
  • 4 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1 ripe avocado, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinaigrette dressing
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Directions

Step 1

Start by thoroughly washing and drying the mixed salad greens. Tear or chop them into bite-sized pieces to create a bed of fresh greens in a large salad bowl. This vibrant foundation will provide the perfect canvas for the array of delicious toppings in your Cobb salad.

Step 2

Arrange the diced cooked chicken, crumbled bacon, chopped hard-boiled eggs, diced avocado, halved cherry tomatoes, crumbled blue cheese, and chopped red onion in rows or sections on top of the salad greens.

Step 3

Drizzle the balsamic vinaigrette dressing over the toppings. Season with a pinch of salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Gently toss the salad just before serving to mix the ingredients and coat them with the dressing.
